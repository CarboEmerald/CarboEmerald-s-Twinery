Not Weird. Queer

Not Weird. Queer is a loosely autobiographical text-based video game about a trans teen going through middle school.

Currently, Not Weird. Queer is still in the alpha-af (pre-alpha) stage of developement.

Version history

Alpha-af

Alpha-af versions are work-in-progresses. They do not include the full story and contain skeletonized dialogue and descriptions. Point systems are subject to change without notice.

  • Version 0.0.002 includes Day 1 and is fully playable. Report bugs and grammar inconsistancies to the discussions pages.

Alpha

Alpha versions are incomplete. They do include the full story line but contain skeletonized dialogue and descriptions. Point systems are subject to change without notice

  • Version 0.1.0 has not been completed yet.

Beta

Beta versions are complete. They include the full story and dialogue, but may still contain bugs. Point values are still subject to change without notice.

  • Version 0.2.0-beta has not been completed yet.

Public release

Public release versions are complete. They come as-is but may include minor bug fixes from time to time.

  • Version 1.0.0 has not been completed yet.
